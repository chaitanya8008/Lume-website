Questions

Before you ask.

Can you make a mirror in a size that isn't listed?

Yes — every mirror is cut to order. Give us your exact width and height in millimetres or inches, and we build to it. There is no fixed size chart.

How do I get a price?

Add designs to your quote list, pick your size, features and frame, then submit. We reply with a personalised quote on WhatsApp or email — usually within a working day. No prices are published because every mirror is custom.

Can I change the light colour after installation?

Always. Tap the touch sensor to cycle cool white → natural white → warm white. Press and hold to dim. The mirror remembers your brightness.

Do you ship across India?

Yes — mirrors are packed for transit and shipped pan-India from our Panchkula workshop. Tell us your city when requesting a quote and we'll include delivery details.

What about IP rating and warranty?

Full IP rating and warranty details are being finalised and will be published here soon. Ask us on WhatsApp in the meantime and we'll share specifics for your order.

I run a glass or mirror business. Can you frost my panels?

Yes — that's our trade service. Send us your own panels and artwork; our fibre laser frosts them at ±0.1 mm precision, in bulk, with fast turnaround. See the trade service.
